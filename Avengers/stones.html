Mind Stone

The Mind Stone is characterized by its deep yellow color and is embedded in the forehead of the android known as Vision. It grants various extraordinary abilities, including enhanced intelligence, mental manipulation, and the ability to control the minds of others.

Reality Stone

The Reality Stone is characterized by its red color and possesses the ability to bend and manipulate reality itself. It has the power to reshape matter, alter the laws of physics, and create illusions that can deceive the senses of those around it.

Power Stone

The Power Stone is characterized by its vibrant purple color and holds an incredible amount of raw energy and destructive power. It has the ability to enhance the strength and abilities of its wielder to an extraordinary degree.

Soul Stone

The Soul Stone is characterized by its deep orange color and possesses the ability to manipulate and control the souls of living beings. Unlike the other stones, the Soul Stone requires a sacrifice to be obtained. In the MCU, this sacrifice involved the one seeking the stone sacrificing someone they love in exchange for its power.

Space Stone

The Space Stone is characterized by its bright blue color and holds dominion over space itself. It grants the wielder the ability to teleport across vast distances, create portals, and manipulate space to their advantage.

Time Stone

The Time Stone is characterized by its bright green color and possesses the ability to manipulate time itself. It grants the wielder control over the flow of time, allowing them to reverse or accelerate events, see into the future, and create time loops.
